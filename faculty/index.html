    Associate Professor

    Biography:

    Dr. Cleary is a Professor of Structural Engineering in the Civil & Environmental Engineering Department at Rowan University. He teaches courses in Structural Engineering as well as engineering mechanics and engineering clinics.     Biography:

    Dr. Everett is a Professor of Environmental Engineering in the Civil & Environmental Engineering Department at Rowan University. He teaches Engineering Clinic, Civil Engineering Systems, Engineering Graphics, Sustainable Civil & Environmental Engineering, Sustainable Technology for the Built Environment, and upper level Environmental Electives. His research interests are in Sustainable Design, Solid and Hazardous Waste Management, Site Remediation, and Education Innovation. Dr. Everett has been at Rowan since Fall 1998.     Beena Sukumaran

    Professor and Chair of Civil & Environmental Engineering

    Biography:

    Beena Sukumaran is a graduate from Purdue University's geotechnical group and her research interests include studying the effect of inherent particle characteristics on shear strength of soils. Her other research interests include the use of discrete element method (DEM) to study the micro-mechanics of granular media and fuzzy set applications in geotechnical engineering. In addition, she also studies the performance of suction caissons for different soil conditions and works with the FAA to study the impact of the next generation of aircrafts on flexible pavements. She directs the research activities at the Soil Mechanics laboratory at Rowan.
