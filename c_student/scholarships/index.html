Suzanne Axworthy Undergraduate Scholarship - 1 award of $1,000, November application deadline. Open to women with a GPA of 3.0 or higher who plan to pursue a career in transportation. Also - Carmen E. Turner Graduate Scholarship. Coordinate with Dr. Sukumaran. -last updated 9/14/06

Audrey Frapaul & Edward O. Davis Memorial Student Summer Employment Scholarship Program –1 award of $5,000, 11 awards of $3,500, 15 of $2,500 (Franklyn Grosso stipend), November application deadline. American Society of Civil Engineers (www.asce.org/inside/stud_chapacts.cfm)

Samuel Fletcher Tapman Scholarship, ~12 at $2,000, February application deadline. Any year, ASCE student member, money must be used to continue undergraduate education. We can only forward one student from Rowan. We’ve won a bunch of these! See website for more information. Coordinate with Dr. Cleary. -last updated 9/14/06

Freeman Fellowship, $2,000-$5,000, February application deadline. Grant for expenses for experiments, observations, and compilations to discover new and accurate data useful in engineering, emphasis on hydraulic science and art. See website for more information. Coordinate with professor who would supervise your activities. -last updated 9/14/06

Daniel W. Mead Contest, 1st place $1000, 2nd $800, 3rd $600, 4th $400, 5th $200, paper may be published in Civil Engineering Magazine, March application deadline. ASCE student member, no more than 2000 word paper on specific topic (see website for topic). See website for more information. Coordinate with professor whose expertise best matches the paper topic. -last updated 9/14/06

Construction Engineering Scholarship and Student Prize, 1 of $?, April application deadline. ASCE student member, money must be used to continue undergraduate education, student must intend to seek employment in the construction industry. See website for more information. Coordinate with Dr. Cleary. -last updated 9/14/06
